Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know about Lounge Asia.

🤝 First time at Lounge?

Q1. Can I come alone?
Yes! Most people come solo. We'll say hi, help you settle in, and introduce you if you'd like. 👋

Q2. Do I need to book?
Yes, RSVP is essential. Please click "Going" on our Meetup event page.

📍Note: If you are not on the RSVP list on Meetup, you may not be able to join.
👉 Follow us on Instagram: @_lounge_asia 📸

Q3. Is there an entry fee?
It depends on the venue. Usually, there is a small cover charge at the door, or a one-drink minimum order at the bar to support the venue. 🥂

Q4. What language can I speak?
English, Japanese, Chinese, Taiwanese, or Korean—all welcome! Use whatever feels most natural. 🗣️

Q5. Can I arrive late or leave early?
Absolutely. It's a casual, drop-in style event. Feel free to come and go as you please.

Q6. Do I need to bring anything?
If the event is at a bar/pub, please bring a valid Photo ID (Passport, Driver's License, etc.). 🆔

Q7. What happens when I arrive?
Look for the Host at the front. Tell us your name, get a name tag (if available), and enjoy the night! 🎉📍

🗣️ Community & Partners

Q1. Is this a dating group or a social club?
Friendship comes first. Our events are for making friends naturally. Aggressive pickup lines or asking for contact info immediately is strictly prohibited.

🚫 Safety Policy: If you feel uncomfortable, please alert the Host or Bar Staff immediately. Harassment will result in immediate removal and a permanent ban from all future events.

Q2. What if I am looking for a partner?
We recommend starting as friends first! Alternatively, try our official app "Matcha Match"—it's designed specifically for safe and smart matching. 💖
Check it out here: Matcha Match

Q3. How can I become a Host or City Leader?
We'd love to have you! We are actively looking for visionary leaders in cities worldwide. It's a great way to build your network and gain leadership skills.

👉 Action: DM us on Instagram to apply!

Q4. I'm a business owner. Can we partner or sponsor?
Absolutely. We collaborate with language schools, agencies, venues, and brands to connect them with our global community.

📸 Contact: Please DM us on Instagram (@_lounge_asia) or email us at info@loungeasia.com.au to discuss opportunities.

Q5. Which cities are you active in?
Currently, we have hubs in Brisbane, Sydney, Melbourne, Tokyo, Fukuoka, and Ho Chi Minh City.

✨ Coming soon: Osaka, Cebu, Singapore, and more!

Q6. Is there an official app?
Yes! We are developing "Matcha Match"—a verified app for safer, smarter connections. 💖
Join the waitlist on our homepage to get early-bird perks!
