About Us

Lounge Asia is a casual networking event where people with roots in Asia can gather and chat in Brisbane. It's a warm space where anyone can feel safe and be themselves, beyond nationality or language.

Our Mission

Our goal isn't just to hold events. It's to nurture connections and deepen the bonds of the East Asian community in Brisbane. Through these connections, we want to make your daily life here more enjoyable and comfortable. We're here to help create opportunities that enrich your life, from meeting new friends to finding business opportunities and a place where you feel you belong.

Let's Create a Movement Together!

Lounge Asia is always looking for passionate individuals, companies, and organizations to help us energize the East Asian community in Brisbane. Our activities thrive on your support.

To Influencers & Creators:

Interested in covering our event? We welcome those who can help us expand our community through their platform. We can invite you to our events, so please feel free to get in touch.

To Venue, Restaurant & Cafe Owners:

Lounge Asia is always seeking new places to meet. Are you interested in hosting an event at a unique location like a BBQ spot, a restaurant, or a cafe? We'd love to hear from venue partners.

To Performers, DJs & Sponsors:

We are open to all forms of collaboration, from cultural performances and DJ sets that light up our events, to drink and food sponsorships that delight our attendees.

Let's work together to make the East Asian community in Brisbane even more exciting and welcoming!
