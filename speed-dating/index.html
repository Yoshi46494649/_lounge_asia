❤️ Our Story

Creating comfort before the first hello

We created Speed Dating Asia because meeting someone who understands your background can feel impossible in a new city. Lounge Asia changes that by centring comfort, respect, and genuine connection at every touchpoint.

Each round is hosted by friendly staff who get the culture — and the nerves. All you need to do is arrive, relax, and enjoy the evening. We'll guide the flow, keep the vibes warm, and celebrate your wins.

Frequently asked questions

New to speed dating or joining us for the first time? Here is everything you need to know before you walk in.

How does the evening work? +

After check-in with photo ID, you will receive your name card and seat. Men rotate every five minutes while women stay seated. Short pauses keep the pace comfortable, and our hosts guide you through each rotation.

Why are votes limited? +

Each guest has four Likes and one Super Like. Limiting votes encourages intentional choices, keeps the playing field fair, and lets real chemistry stand out.

What if I do not match with anyone? +

It happens, and that is okay. Many guests meet their match at a later round. We also keep match results private unless they are mutual, so the experience stays positive.

When will I receive my results? +

We tally preferences digitally and email mutual matches to both participants the following evening.

Can I bring friends? +

Absolutely. Just remind them to register individually so we can keep the numbers balanced.

How do you keep the event safe? +

Every guest is ID-verified on arrival. No contact details are exchanged during the event, and our team is on-site to uphold a zero-tolerance policy for poor behaviour. Anyone who breaks the rules is removed without refund.

Can I cancel my registration? +

You can cancel before payment. Once payment is confirmed the seat is held exclusively for you and cannot be refunded because places are limited each round.
