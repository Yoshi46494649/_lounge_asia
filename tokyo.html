Common Questions

Brisbane Asian Meetup FAQ

Everything you need to know about our Brisbane events.

🔰 First time at Lounge?

Q1. Can I come alone?
Yes! Most people come solo. We’ll say hi, help you settle in, and introduce you if you’d like. E

Q2. Do I need to book?
Yes, Meetup RSVP is STRICTLY REQUIRED. 🎟EE
No RSVP = No Entry. We check the Meetup attendance list at the door. Please ensure you click "Attend" on our Meetup page before arriving.

Q3. Is there an entry fee?
Entry requires either:
1E⃣ Purchase of at least 1 drink at the bar, OR
2E⃣ $5 cash entry fee at the door.
Thank you for supporting our venue and the community! 🍺

Q4. What language can I speak?
English, Japanese, Chinese, Taiwanese, or Korean—all welcome! Use whatever feels most natural. 🌏

Q5. Can I arrive late or leave early?
Absolutely. It’s a casual, drop-in style event. Feel free to come and go as you please.

Q6. Do I need to bring anything?
If the event is at a bar/pub, please bring a valid Photo ID (Passport, Driver’s License, etc.). E

🛡EE/span> Safety & Community

Q7. What are the House Rules?
We have a Zero Tolerance Policy to keep our space safe:
🚫 No Solicitation: No MLM, business recruitment, or poaching.
🚫 No Harassment: No pickup artists or creepy behavior.
Violators are removed immediately. If you ever feel uncomfortable, please tell the Host.

Q8. What is "VIP Access" for Founding Members?
Founding Members gain early access to our official app and special party invites. Join the VIP list to be part of the inner circle as we grow! 🎁

Q9. Is this a dating group or a social club?
Friendship first. Our events are for making friends naturally. For those looking for a partner, we recommend our official app "Matcha Match"—it’s designed specifically for verified matching. 📲

Lounge Asia is Tokyo's premier destination for connecting with global-minded Asians, returnees, and international professionals. Our meetups near Tokyo Station, Nihonbashi, and Marunouchi provide a safe and high-quality 'Third Place' for everyone to build genuine connections.
